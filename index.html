About the Developer

Hi, I’m Jana — a Computer Engineering student at TU Berlin with a passion for technology and helping others.

As an international student myself, I know how overwhelming it can be to manage deadlines, visa appointments, and settle into a new country. That’s why I created this app — to make life a little easier for others going through the same journey.

When I’m not coding, I’m probably organizing something no one asked for, giving TED-style speeches to my mirror, or convincing myself I have time for just one more side project.

This project is part of my mission to blend tech with impact — and this is just the beginning.
